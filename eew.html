Get Notified

When an Earthquake occurs in Japan, the program will trigger a notification, and will play a chime to get your attention. The combination of the notification and chime are benificial to grabbing the user's attention. The notification provides basic information about the Earthquake, and the chime is there to alert you of the Notification & Earthquake.

What to do

    You've just received an Earthquake Early Warning. Now what?

  • Don't panic.

    • Creating panic will make the situation worse, and can lead to injury.

  • Determine if you are near the Epicenter of the Earthquake.

    • If you don't live in an area that is near the Earthquake's Epicenter, you should be fine. If the Epicenter is near you, or you think the Earthquake may affect your area,

  • Move away from tall furniture, or anything that could possibly falling on you.
  • Open a door or window.

    • Incase their frames become buckled in the quake and you are unable to escape.

  • Hide under a table, or sit on the floor in a hurder position with your hands rested on your head.

    • Patiently wait for the Earthquake to pass.
